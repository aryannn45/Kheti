Assured Contract Farming System by Kheti

Kheti, a pioneering name in agricultural solutions, has introduced its Assured Contract Farming System, designed to revolutionize the farming landscape. This system is built on the principles of mutual trust, fair trade, and sustainable agricultural practices. It provides farmers with a guaranteed market for their produce, ensuring steady income and reducing the uncertainties associated with traditional farming.
Under this system, Kheti enters into pre-agreed contracts with farmers, specifying the type of crop, cultivation practices, and procurement price. This transparency eliminates market volatility and assures farmers of a fixed return on their investment. The company also extends support throughout the crop cycle, offering high-quality seeds, fertilizers, and expert agronomic advice. This integrated approach not only boosts productivity but also enhances the quality of the yield. With its Assured Contract Farming System, Kheti not only enhances the livelihoods of farmers but also contributes to food security and rural development. This model reflects Kheti’s commitment to building a resilient agricultural ecosystem where farmers thrive as essential stakeholders in the supply chain.p>
